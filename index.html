About Me

CS student, data enthusiast, lifelong tinkerer.

I'm currently studying Computer Science with a focus on data science, spending most of my time at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and software engineering. I like problems where the answer isn't obvious — where you have to dig through the data first to even know what question to ask.

Outside of coursework, I build side projects to learn things the classroom doesn't cover fast enough — usually that means picking a dataset I'm curious about and seeing what I can find. I care more about getting to a correct, well-reasoned answer than about using the trendiest tool to get there.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably reading about a new ML paper, helping a classmate debug something, or losing more games of chess than I'd like to admit.
